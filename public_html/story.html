-Our story

Lüna stems from a lineage of carpenters, architects and designers. By creating bespoke designs through the use of fine natural materials, crafted and presented with precision, we seek to embody authentic design in every-day life with a unique edge. Since 2014 Lüna has expanded it’s focus from interior design to the production of bespoke furniture, most notably in the hospitality industry.

With a fascination for material exploration and through testing the performance of various natural materials, the founder’s love of wood design developed. Every item is created using the finest materials and the highest attention to detail, resulting in durable products of undisputed quality.

Come and try our designs for yourself, we are sure you are going to love them.
